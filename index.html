Our Mission

Computer science education at Texas State University is being held back by the ways in which it is taught. Traditional lectures ignore the growth of critical thinking and collaboration skills, leaving students unprepared for careers where these skills are vitally important. We have researched how new methods of computer science education can enrich student learning and better prepare them for their future endeavours.
